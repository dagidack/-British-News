          Daily Feed News UK is a UK news aggregator that brings together public RSS feeds from national broadcasters, newspapers, and regional publishers across Britain.

          Our aim is a clear, fast way to follow stories from London to Glasgow — national headlines and local reporting in one continuous feed on the home page.

          We do not write our own journalism. Every headline links to the original publisher where you can read the full article.
